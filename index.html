Sardar Patel Institute Technology

 

Sardar Patel Institute Technology is just like most engineering colleges out there in Mumbai or even the country.

The best of city lads get admission here, but they are just like any of your school friends with whom you have done crazy and stupid stuff.

Talking of teaching standards , I am sorry to mention but its below average. Well this is my honest opinion, there might be others with different viewpoint.

But you can still survive here, it takes some more active neurons, that’s it.
One great advantage is you can be anything you want to be. There is lot of flexibility in student programs and events. Students take initiative to do many good things themselves and there are least hurdles to clear it. Though teachers are not up to the mark, they neither are pain if you are doing something innovative, knowledge and talent is respected
